Welcome to Reservoir Calculation for DR App!

This app allows you to calculate the reservoir boudary and volume with a designed pour point and water level in Dominican Republic country. Detailed steps includes:

First, you can zoom in/out of the map to find a pour point, then click on the map to draw a point. Then, you can draw a polygon on the map to define the maximum reservior boundary. (Note: You should click on the buttons everytime you want to draw a new point or polygon)

Next, you can input a water level at the pour point, click "Calculate" button to run.

The formed reservoir boundary will show on the map and its volume will show as text below the "Calculate" button.

* Please make sure to select an outlet within the Dominican Republic Country.
